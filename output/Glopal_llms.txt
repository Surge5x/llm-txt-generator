# Glopal
> Glopal provides services for e-commerce businesses to expand globally through automated store localization, international marketing, and compliance for taxes and duties. It includes translations, cross-border shipping, and trade compliance.

## Services
- [Home](https://merchants.glopal.com/en-us/home): Overview of Glopal's international ecommerce services, including localization, marketing, and tax compliance.
- [International Marketing](https://merchants.glopal.com/en-us/attract): Details services for attracting global buyers through Google, Facebook, Instagram, and SEO.
- [International Facebook & Instagram Ads](https://merchants.glopal.com/en-us/solutions/international-facebook-ads): Automates localization of product catalogs and ads for global Facebook and Instagram campaigns.
- [International Google Shopping Ads](https://merchants.glopal.com/en-us/solutions/international-google-shopping): Automates localization of product feeds and campaigns for global Google Shopping.
- [International SEO](https://merchants.glopal.com/en-us/solutions/international-seo): Generates indexable, localized store versions in multiple languages and currencies for improved international organic search visibility.
