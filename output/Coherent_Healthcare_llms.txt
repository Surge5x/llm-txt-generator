# Coherent Healthcare

> Coherent Healthcare provides AI-powered patient retention and seamless payment solutions to medical practices, enabling proactive patient follow-up and streamlined billing.

## Products

- [Sales Desk](https://coherenthq.com/sales-desk): A human-supervised AI patient coordinator service for proactive patient follow-up, lead management, post-consultation engagement, and patient recall.
- [Billing Desk](https://coherenthq.com/payments): A healthcare payment solution to manage all billing and payments, including card, bank, and digital wallet options, for remote and in-person transactions.
